Outpatient Department (OPD)

The Outpatient Department (OPD) serves as the first point of contact between patients and the hospital. It provides medical consultation, diagnosis, and treatment for patients who do not require admission or overnight stay.

Key Functions of OPD

  • Initial medical examination and diagnosis
  • Referral to specialists when required
  • Routine follow-up consultations
  • Preventive healthcare and wellness checkups

Medical Specialties Available

  • General Medicine
  • Orthopedics
  • Pediatrics
  • Gynecology
  • Dermatology
  • ENT and Ophthalmology

Facilities & Infrastructure

  • Patient registration and appointment counters
  • Comfortable waiting areas
  • Electronic Health Record (EHR) system
  • Wheelchair and elderly support services

Working Hours

Monday to Saturday: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM

Sunday: Closed (Emergency services available)
