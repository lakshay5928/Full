Emergency Department

The Emergency Department provides immediate and life-saving medical care to patients with critical conditions. It operates round-the-clock and is equipped to handle all types of medical emergencies.

Emergency Services

  • Accident and trauma management
  • Heart attack and cardiac emergencies
  • Stroke and neurological emergencies
  • Burns, poisoning, and fractures

Advanced Facilities

  • Fully equipped ambulances
  • Emergency operation theatres
  • Intensive Care Units (ICU)
  • Ventilators and life-support systems

Medical Team

  • Emergency physicians
  • Trauma surgeons
  • Critical care nurses
  • Paramedical staff

Availability

24 Hours × 7 Days
