About Us

Welcome to our online shopping platform. This website is a demo e-commerce applicationdeveloped for educational and learning purposes only. The platform is inspired by popular online marketplaces such as Amazon.

Our goal is to demonstrate the basic working of an e-commerce system, including product listing, user interaction, and order management. All products, prices, descriptions, and contact details shown on this website are dummy data and do not represent any real business or organization.

This project helps students understand concepts such as website design, database handling, backend integration, and user experience in online shopping systems.

No real transactions are performed on this platform, and no real user data is collected or stored. This project strictly follows academic and ethical guidelines.
