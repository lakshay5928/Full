Diagnostic Laboratory

The Diagnostic Laboratory plays a vital role in supporting doctors by providing accurate medical test results. It helps in early detection, diagnosis, and monitoring of diseases using advanced diagnostic technology.

Diagnostic Services Offered

  • Blood tests (CBC, sugar, cholesterol)
  • Urine and stool analysis
  • X-Ray and Digital Radiography
  • Ultrasound imaging
  • CT Scan and ECG

Laboratory Features

  • State-of-the-art diagnostic equipment
  • Automated testing systems
  • Highly trained lab technicians
  • Quick and accurate reporting

Patient Facilities

  • Sample collection rooms
  • Online test report access
  • Home sample collection services
  • Proper hygiene and safety protocols

Timings

All days: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM
